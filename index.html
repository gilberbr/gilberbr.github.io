Midnight: A Cinderella Retelling

Midnight: A Cinderella Retelling releases August 13th on all platforms! This fantasy retelling of the beloved fairytale is more twisted than the original. The characters range from Fae to witches to all sorts of fantasy creatures!

Reviews for Midnight

Lynn Shaw from 2 Girls & A Book wrote:

Melanie Gilbert creates a symbolic retelling of Cinderella in her first Fae Brothers' Ever After Novel, Midnight. While there is no glass slipper or evil step-mother, the cast of Midnight is representative of the classic story as it is told in this modern fantasy. With the introduction of fae, shifters, and other magics, Gilbert delivers a unique twist on this retelling. The characters are diverse and multi-dimensional, each with their own story waiting to be told. Did I mention that the fellas are hot? Well, they are smoking hot! And what fairytale is complete without a villain? Gilbert's villain is a ruthless snake but you have to have to admire her tenacity. Midnight is a well-written, fast paced fantasy novel. I cannot wait to see what Gilbert's take is on the other fairytales she tackles in this series.
