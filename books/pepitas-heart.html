Pepita's Heart

Pepita Anderson is a Magic Binder in search of a Heart, or soulmate. When she turns sixteen, she only has three years to find him. If she doesn't find him in time, she won't be able to combine their magic, and with Pepita's magic almost inaccessible to her, she could use all the help she can get. At her mother's insistence, they travel to Alaska to aid in her search, but instead must face evil creatures called Sifters who have declared war on the Magic Binders and attack the Magical Vortex they are tasked to protect.

Kolisis Sharp doesn't want a Heart. With one week left before his nineteenth birthday he believes he's in the clear, despite his parents' meddling. When Kole meets Pepita, sparks fly, but not the kind their parents had hoped for. He wants her gone and vows to spend every second trying to make that a reality. Angry, heated sparks between them threaten to melt the snowy Alaskan frontier.

With the threat of the Sifters, Kole and Pepita must put their differences aside. Can Kole come to terms with his denial of a Heart in time? Will Pepita be able to strengthen her magic to defeat the evil threatening them? Or will the Sifters win, gaining control of the Alaskan Vortex and throwing the world into Chaos?
